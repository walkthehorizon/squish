Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

PhotoSquish ("we") respects and protects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect what is necessary for the app to function:

  • Photo library access: Used so you can select images to compress. We do not upload or store your original photos.
  • Local storage: Compression history (e.g. file names, sizes, ratios, timestamps) and output file paths are stored only on your device for the "Works" list and history.

2. How We Use Information

  • Selected images are compressed only on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • You may save or share compressed results yourself; we do not upload or share them on your behalf.

3. Storage and Security

Temporary files and history are stored in the app's private directory on your device; other apps cannot access them. We do not send your images or personal data to third-party servers.

4. Permissions

  • Read photo library: To choose images to compress.
  • Save to photo library: To save compressed images to your gallery when you tap "Save."

5. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated policy will be published in the app. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via the in-app "Contact" option.
